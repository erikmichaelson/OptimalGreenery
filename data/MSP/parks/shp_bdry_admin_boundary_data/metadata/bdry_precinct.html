Section 1 Overview
Originator Ramsey County
Title Election Precincts, Ramsey County, Minnesota
Abstract This data set contains polygons that represent Ramsey County Precincts as of the redistricting process following the 2010 census. Since the 2010 census some precincts boundaries have been updated in Little Canada, Maplewood, New Brighton, and St Paul as directed by city resolutions.
Purpose Precincts are often used as boundaries for analyzing other GIS data, including election districts, census data, tax data, and county services.

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy Attributes are populated from a compilation of records and information from various state, county and city offices, and other sources. Accuracy may vary by source, but domains are used wherever possible to increase accuracy.
Logical Consistency Graphical elements are topologically clean. Over shoots and undershoots are not permitted. Parcel gaps and overlaps are analyzed in a case by case basis. Generally boundaries conflicting in position by less than 1 foot are dissolved into a single line giving precedence to Senior and Junior property boundaries
Completeness Entire Ramsey County Coverage
Horizontal Positional Accuracy Digitized information is generally +/- 7 feet; some areas have been found to be +/- 30 feet. COGO (coordinate geometry) accuracy generally is +/- 1.0 foot.
Lineage The Ramsey County Precincts were created as part of a redistricting process following the 2010 census and subsequent city resolutions.
