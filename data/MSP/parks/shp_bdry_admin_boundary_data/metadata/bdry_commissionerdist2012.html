Section 1 Overview
Originator Ramsey County
Title County Commissioner Districts, Ramsey County, Minnesota
Abstract This dataset contains polygons that represent Ramsey County commissioner districts as of the redistricting process following the 2010 census.
Purpose Commissioner districts are often used as boundaries for analyzing other GIS data, including census data, tax data, and county services.
Time Period of Content Date 2012
Currentness Reference Commissioner districts are modified following political re-districting activities. Attribution may also be changed following election of new commissioners.

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy Attributes are populated from a compilation of records and information from various state, county and city offices, and other sources. Accuracy may vary by source, but domains are used wherever possible to increase accuracy.
Logical Consistency Information is visually inspected and corrected as necessary.
Completeness Entire Ramsey County coverage
Horizontal Positional Accuracy Digitized information is generally +/- 7 feet; some areas have been found to be +/- 30 feet. COGO (coordinate geometry) accuracy generally is +/- 1.0 foot.
Lineage The Ramsey County commissioner districts were created as part of a redistricting process following the 2010 census. The boundaries were created by merging Federal Census Blocks which had individually been assigned to a commissioner district.
