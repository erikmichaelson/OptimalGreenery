Section 1 Overview
Originator Ramsey County
Title Parks and Recreation Areas, Ramsey County, Minnesota
Abstract This dataset contains polygons that represent park boundaries in Ramsey County, including golf courses, open spaces and recreational centers. The parks boundaries are compiled from a variety of sources including Ramsey County Parks & Recreation department and several municipalities.
Purpose Park boundaries are used in the production of mapping products as well as online applications.
Time Period of Content Date Publication Date
Currentness Reference The dataset is current and complete, but regularly updated to reflect changes. New versions are released monthly.

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy Attributes are populated from a compilation of records and information from various state, county and city offices, and other sources. Accuracy may vary by source, but domains are used wherever possible to increase accuracy.
Logical Consistency Information is visually inspected and corrected as necessary.
Completeness Entire Ramsey County coverage
Horizontal Positional Accuracy Digitized information is generally +/- 7 feet; some areas have been found to be +/- 30 feet. COGO (coordinate geometry) accuracy generally is +/- 1.0 foot.
